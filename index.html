The story behind the CV

In 2025, I was in the middle of a deliberate career switch — stepping out of a long run at TCS to build something new — when my mother faced a serious health emergency that required intensive, ongoing care. I made the decision to move back home to Lucknow, even though it meant stepping away from opportunities I'd built elsewhere.

That period taught me more about resilience and priorities than any project ever has. I found Megasoft shortly after — a role that let me build a real analytics career while staying close enough to manage hospital visits and look after my mother. TCS gave me the space I needed before that transition, and Megasoft has given me the flexibility to keep showing up for my family while doing serious, meaningful work.

I don't lead with this story in interviews, but it's the reason I work the way I do: with focus, without wasted motion, and with a clear sense of what actually matters when the pressure is real.
